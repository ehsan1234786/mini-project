Why resume is important?

1. First Impression: A resume is often the first impression an employer has of you.
It's a snapshot of your qualifications and experiences, and it can make or break your chances of getting an interview.

2. Demonstrates Qualifications: Your resume highlights your skills, qualifications, and relevant experiences.
It provides an overview of your educational background, work history, and any certifications or training you've received.

3.Professionalism: A well-organized and well-written resume demonstrates professionalism and attention to detail.
It shows that you've put thought and effort into your job application.
